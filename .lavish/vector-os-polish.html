Under the hood

the quiet wins

The changes that don't show up in a screenshot but matter most for quality.

Perf · hero canvas
The film-grain rewrote every viewport pixel every frame. Now it renders a small noise buffer scaled up (chunky CRT grain), capped to 30fps, and paused when off-screen or the tab is hidden.
Perf · hero tilt
The parallax loop called setTilt() every frame, re-rendering the whole hero ~60fps even at rest. It now writes the transform straight to the DOM and parks itself once settled.
A11y · reduced motion
Reduced-motion was CSS-only. A useReducedMotion() hook now gates every JS effect — canvas, tilt, scramble, count-up, boot sequences — so they snap to final values.
A11y · focus + nav
One global :focus-visible cyan ring across the site; mobile nav gets Escape, outside-click close and body-scroll-lock; brand selection colour.
System · tokens
Added easing, glow and surface tokens to the @theme. Off-token near-blacks (#0d0d0d, #050505, #030303) and repeated cyan-glow rgba literals now resolve through tokens.
System · layout
A shared <Section> + <Container> replaces the copy-pasted shell string across 6 sections; the navbar breakpoint is aligned to the 900px content system; 15 keyframes registered.
